DailyStream

A minimalist photo timeline app that transforms a photo library into a visual journey through memories - one photo per day.

Visit Live Site

About This Project

The app presents photos in a clean calendar-style grid, list with a preview as well as highlighting "On This Day" memories from past years. Users can seamlessly switch between timeline, list, and gallery views, and open any day to browse the photos captured during that moment in time.

There is a companion app written in Flutter (for Android and iOS) that allows user to upload photos from their phone to the app (via phone's 'sharing' feature). A custom Filestorage backend (used by my other apps, as it's a multitenant solution) is used to store the uploaded photos and generate thumbnails for them.
